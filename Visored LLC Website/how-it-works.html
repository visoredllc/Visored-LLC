Payment

Payment starts before full development.

The free demo helps you see the possible direction first. If you decide to move forward, payment is handled before full website development begins. Flexible financing options may be available depending on the project.

Monthly Care

Sites need ongoing support after launch.

The Advanced Growth Package requires a $49.99 monthly site operations plan. This supports hosting help, form checks, basic upkeep, and general site availability.

Updates

Your site can keep changing with your business.

After the completed review window, new changes can still be requested as paid add-ons. This can include flyers, sales, photos, content updates, business changes, and new features.
